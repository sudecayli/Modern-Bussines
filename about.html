Our story

CYL The Irish Coffee Company was founded in 1990 by Sude Cayli – to this day Sude and the team at CYL the Irish Coffee Company remain dedicated to providing the best Irish Cafe Concepts throughout the world.Our story begins in 1973 and Sude’s first encounter with the design of Irish Cafes through a College Project on the ‘Best Designs within Dublin Cafes’

Growth & Beyond

We packaged the Irish Cafe for export – This became the CYL Irish Cafe Concept – the same concept that every CYL Irish Cafe Designer uses today. In the early 1990’s the Irish Cafe expanded into Europe and from 1995 onwards, it expanded into the USA and throughout the rest of the world. To this day – The Irish Pub Company remains the leader in CYL Irish Cafe Design.
